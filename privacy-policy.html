Privacy Policy.

Your privacy and data security are fundamental to our mission. Learn how we protect your personal information and use AI responsibly.

Last Updated: December 30, 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

AI First Credit Union is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our members' personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our services, visit our website, or interact with us in any way.

Key Privacy Principles

  • We collect only the information necessary to provide our services
  • We use AI ethically and transparently to enhance your banking experience
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • You have control over your data and privacy settings

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you:

  • Apply for membership or open an account
  • Apply for loans or other financial products
  • Use our online or mobile banking services
  • Contact us for customer service
  • Participate in surveys or promotional activities

This may include your name, address, phone number, email address, Social Security number, date of birth, employment information, income, and financial account information.

Transaction Information

We collect information about your transactions and account activity, including deposits, withdrawals, transfers, payments, and other account activities.

Technical Information

When you use our digital services, we may collect technical information such as your IP address, device type, browser information, operating system, and usage patterns to improve our services and ensure security.

How We Use Your Information

Account Services

  • • Process transactions and maintain accounts
  • • Provide customer service and support
  • • Send account statements and notifications
  • • Verify your identity and prevent fraud

AI-Powered Enhancements

  • • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities
  • • Provide personalized financial insights
  • • Improve our products and services
  • • Enhance user experience and security

Legal & Regulatory

  • • Comply with federal and state regulations
  • • Report suspicious activities as required
  • • Respond to legal requests and subpoenas
  • • Maintain required records and documentation

Communication

  • • Send important account information
  • • Provide educational resources
  • • Offer relevant products and services
  • • Conduct surveys and research

AI and Machine Learning

We use artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies to enhance our services while maintaining strict ethical standards:

Ethical AI Practices

  • Transparency: We explain how AI affects decisions that impact you
  • Human Oversight: All AI decisions are subject to human review and appeal
  • Bias Prevention: We actively monitor and correct for algorithmic bias
  • Member Control: You can opt out of AI-driven features at any time

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who help us provide services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Fraud Prevention: To prevent, detect, or investigate fraud or other illegal activities
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with your consent)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • • Advanced firewall and intrusion detection systems
  • • Regular security audits and penetration testing

Operational Safeguards

  • • Employee background checks and security training
  • • Strict access controls and monitoring
  • • Secure data centers with physical security
  • • Regular backup and disaster recovery procedures

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability

Request a copy of your personal information and data portability

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)

Opt-Out

Opt out of marketing communications and AI-driven features

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website and mobile applications. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and sending you a notice if required by law.
