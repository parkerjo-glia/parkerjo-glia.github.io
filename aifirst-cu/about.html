Our Story

AI First Credit Union was created in 2026 as a demonstration site by Glia Technologies, Inc. The "AI First Council" referenced throughout this site is a fictional entity created solely for demo purposes—it does not represent a real organization.

This demo showcases how a modern credit union might leverage AI-powered customer service and digital engagement tools. The site demonstrates Glia's integration capabilities for financial institutions looking to enhance their member experience.

While the credit union depicted here is fictional, the technology and customer engagement features demonstrated are real capabilities offered by Glia to financial institutions worldwide.

Our Mission & Values

The principles that guide this demo experience.
