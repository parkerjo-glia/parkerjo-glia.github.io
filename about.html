Our Story

Founded in 2023 by members of the AI First Council, AI First Credit Union was born from a vision to create a financial institution that embraces the potential of artificial intelligence while maintaining the human-centered values that define credit unions.

As technology professionals, researchers, and innovators, our founding members recognized the need for a financial partner that understood both the opportunities and responsibilities that come with AI advancement. We set out to build a credit union that would serve as a model for ethical AI implementation in financial services.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of members who share our commitment to responsible innovation, financial transparency, and community-driven banking.

Ethical AI Implementation

We use AI to enhance member experiences while maintaining strict ethical guidelines, transparency, and human oversight in all automated decisions.

Member-Owned Structure

As a credit union, we're owned by our members, not shareholders. This means better rates, lower fees, and decisions made in your best interest.
