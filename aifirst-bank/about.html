Our Story

Built for Demonstration

AI First Bank was created in 2026 as a demonstration site by Glia Technologies, Inc. This fictional bank showcases how modern financial institutions can leverage AI-powered customer service and digital engagement tools.

The site demonstrates Glia's integration capabilities for banks looking to enhance their customer experience through intelligent automation and personalized service.

While the bank depicted here is fictional, the technology and customer engagement features demonstrated are real capabilities offered by Glia to financial institutions worldwide.
